Our heavy duty tarpaulin sheets are manufactured from high-density polyethylene (HDPE) with UV stabilizers, ensuring long-lasting performance in harsh outdoor conditions. These waterproof covers provide excellent protection against rain, sun, dust, and wind.

The tarpaulins feature reinforced edges with rust-proof aluminum grommets every 3 feet for easy tying and securing. The material is tear-resistant and flexible even in cold weather.

Perfect for trucks, construction sites, agriculture, camping, and emergency coverings. Available in blue, green, orange, and silver colors.
